About me

I am a candidate for Bachelor of Mathematics at the University of Waterloo. My interests include website development and design. Since I was a kid, my curiousity for how things work led to a passion for math and solving complex problems. My background in Music has also enhanced my ability to learn independently. After years of being classically trained in the piano, I experimented applying the same principals in music theory to teach myself to play guitar. Since then, I have entered a rabbithole of self-learning. I continue to learn different skills in music and new skills including photography and programming.

With a love for technical thinking, I joined my high school's robotics team and climbed the ranks to become president of the club and leader of the teams. I competed in 2 FIRST Lego League competitions on a national level and a FIRST Global Challenge in 2021. My team and I, Team Trinidad and Tobago, placed first in our respective category. These experiences have allowed me to showcase and expand my abilities in leadership, communication and management. As I continue my studies, I hope to achieve a career in software development and continue to discover new skills.
